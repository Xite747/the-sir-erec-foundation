EREC THE XVIII

Our All Powerful And All Mighty God

Long ago there was a mighty being known as Erec The XVIII. He used to be a normal boy until the demons came to his village and devoured all of the inhabitants including his dear sister. After this mighty carnage, he had experienced he saw a truly horrifying truth. He was the only survivor, even his dear village was flattened to the ground like nothing even had ever been there, not even trees or grass. Just grief, hatred and despair..

Erec The XVIII


After this horrible experience our All Mighty God Erec The XVIII set out on a great journey to become the God we know, love and worship today. His journey wasn't easy and it was filled with hatred, anger, sadness, disparity amongst the people which lit wars and chaos throughout the realm he resided in. He got stronger and stronger every day, and finally the day he had been training for dozens of years now had finally come at last. He set out to fight the Demon Gods who were responsible for the deaths of millions of innocent peasants. He fought bravely and valiantly like a warrior of the true Legendary Heros of the 7 Legendary Scrolls. The fight had exhausted him greatly to the brink of death. The people believed in his might and right before his ascension to heaven his home he cried out to the people.

Thou people of mine realm heed the words of God I shalt lay upon you. I have slain this evil from our holy land. For thou shalt remember I as Erec The XVIII, The Mighty God who saved your souls from these snakes. And thou shalt Rejoice this day every year to remember the God of your land. Thou shalt write a religious text of I and praise it, you shall also believe in and pray to I and I shall give you my blessing. For now, I must ascend to heaven but remember, one day 2 great heroes shall rise to make I known to the world again. Do not forget this, live by me and you shall be saved.
